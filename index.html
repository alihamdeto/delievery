Coronavirus – COVID 19 Update Tuesday 24 March

CEO Update.

As we continue to navigate this unprecedented situation, Cash Converters stores will temporarily close in UK in line with government guidance. All our UK stores and Cash Converters online, will no longer be open for business from today 24 March 2020, to protect the safety and well-being of not only staff and their families, but also our customers and local communities.

We would like to thank all of our Cash Converters’ colleagues for their hard work and dedication during this challenging time. Their efforts have been invaluable and we’re incredibly thankful to them for continuously supporting their communities up until this point.

We hope our staff and our customers remain safe and healthy during this time and look forward to welcoming you all back to our stores in the future.

If you have your items with us using either our Pawnbroking or BuyBack services, these will be kept safe during this difficult time and will be available to you when we reopen.  If you need to contact us, please email customerservices.uk@cashconverters.com

Peter Hall – CEO UK
